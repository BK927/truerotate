# Privacy Policy

**TrueRotate** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

TrueRotate runs entirely on your own computer. It does not connect to the
internet, contains no analytics or telemetry, and shows no ads. The only data it
stores is a local settings file (your hotkeys and preferences) at
`%AppData%\TrueRotate\config.json` on your device. This file never leaves your
computer.

Because TrueRotate collects no data, there is nothing for the developer to
access, use, or disclose to third parties.

If you have any questions, please open an issue at
<https://github.com/BK927/true-rotate/issues>.
